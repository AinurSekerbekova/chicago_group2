Research purpose

With this research we want to get in touch with citizens and tourists and show them how the city of Chicago changed because of the Great Fire and how it affected the life of the population.

Research Question

"How can interactive digital storytelling using survivor narratives reshape public understanding and emotional engagement with historical urban disasters such as the Great Chicago Fire?"
