Welcome to the Experience

Together, we invite you to experience the Great Chicago Fire. You’ll start in present-day Chicago, solve clues to find historic locations, and watch as each correct guess transforms the cityscape, letting you witness scenes from 1871 and listen to survivor stories. Your choices reveal loss, courage, and rebirth, tying the past to the present.

As you journey, your actions turn modern Chicago into its historic self. Guess today’s locations, and watch them change before your eyes!
